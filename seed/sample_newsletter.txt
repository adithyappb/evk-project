Subject: Weekly STEM Opportunities Digest — 3 picks this week

Hi friends,

Three hand-picked opportunities for students this week. Apply early!

1) NASA JPL Summer Internship 2026
NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory is accepting applications for its 10-week
paid summer internship. Work alongside mission engineers on active spacecraft
and Earth-science programs. Open to U.S. citizens currently enrolled in an
accredited university (undergrad or grad). Locations: Pasadena, CA.
Deadline: February 28, 2026.
Apply: https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/edu/intern/apply/

2) Hack the North 2026 (Waterloo, Canada)
Canada's largest hackathon returns — 1,500+ students, 36 hours, fully-funded
travel for many international attendees. University and college students
worldwide are welcome (18+). Build something cool with mentors from Google,
Stripe, and more. Deadline to apply: August 15, 2026.
More info: https://hackthenorth.com/

3) Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program 2026
Free two-week virtual program for rising 10th-12th grade girls and
non-binary students. Learn web development, UX, and data science from
industry professionals. Application deadline: March 15, 2026.
https://girlswhocode.com/programs/summer-immersion-program

Good luck!
